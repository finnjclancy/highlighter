Welcome to Highlighter

You're ready to go. Here are the four things to know.

1

Select text on any web page

A small toolbar appears with your colour palette. Click any swatch to highlight the selection in that colour. Highlights save automatically and reappear when you revisit the page.

2

Hover or click a highlight to manage it

Hover any highlight for a quick mini-toolbar (re-colour, remove). Click it to open the full popover with the comment field, tag editor, and a button to jump back to the source.

3

Open the library to organise everything

Click the Highlighter icon in your toolbar, then Open library / design. Sort into folders (tags), search across all sites, batch select, and export selections as plain text or Markdown.

4

Share a page's highlights

Extension icon → Share live link. Copies a URL that, when opened, shows your highlights as a clean gallery — and if the recipient also has Highlighter installed, they can open the source page with your highlights painted on it.
